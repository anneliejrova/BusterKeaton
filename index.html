BUSTER KEATON FILM FESTIVAL

A Weekend of Silent Comedy Magic

Step into the world of cinematic genius at the Buster Keaton Film Festival, a weekend celebration of timeless silent films that still make audiences laugh, gasp, and marvel.

Experience two of Keaton's most legendary works, The General and Steamboat Bill, Jr. brought to life with live musical accompaniment, making every chase, stunt, and pratfall more thrilling than ever.

Whether you're a lifelong fan or discovering Keaton for the first time, this festival promises a weekend of laughter, artistry, and pure cinematic joy. Don't miss your chance to witness comedy that has shaped the movies we love today.
